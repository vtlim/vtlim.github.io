Science

University of California, Irvine

2015-2020

I completed my PhD at UC Irvine where I did research in the field of computer-aided drug design. I applied and developed computational tools to study biomolecular systems, such as protein-ligand complexes and membrane proteins. The motivation of my work was to obtain insights from models and simulations to further the development of new therapeutics. I was co-advised by David Mobley and Doug Tobias while at UCI.

You can visit my research bio page here, and check out my dissertation here.

My dissertation defense presentation is also available online here.

Tech Transfer

UCI Invention Transfer Group

2018-2020

Technology transfer is the process of transferring technology from origin to application. The technology transfer office at UC Irvine (Invention Transfer Group, ITG) helps faculty, physicians, and researchers process invention disclosures, patent innovations, and connect to industry partners. As a fellow at UCI ITG, I contributed to the technology transfer process by conducting technology assessments and prior art searches, writing invention summaries and non-confidential disclosures, and initiating market research of UCI technologies.

Technologies I've worked on include
  • Medical devices
  • Polymer chemistry
  • Electrochemical membranes
  • ... and many more!

Communication

Lead Managing Editor, Science Writer

The Loh Down on Science

(2018-2020) I wrote and edited scripts for the radio show and podcast, "The Loh Down on Science." The program, catered towards lay audiences, explains the latest scientific research with a dash of humor in short segments of less than two minutes each. The show is broadcasted on Southern California Public Radio (KPCC) and National Public Radio (NPR). See my LDOS bio page and scripts here.

Communications Consultant

UC Irvine Graduate Resource Center

(2017-2019) I worked one-on-one with UCI graduate students, postdoctoral scholars, alumni, and staff to assist with communication skills. I've helped people prepare for exam presentations, general audience presentations, job interviews, English-speaking exams, and networking events. See my short blurb on the UCI Chemistry Instagram!

Outreach volunteer

Various Events

I enjoy communicating my work to a wide variety of audiences, from the technical comp chem community to more general elementary-age audiences. I've volunteered for the UCI Physical Sciences middle school outreach program (LEAPS) as well as the American Association for University Women's Tech Trek.
