Science

I am a fourth year graduate student at UC Irvine, working in the field of computer-aided drug design. In this line of research, I apply computational tools to study biomolecular systems, such as protein-ligand complexes. The motivation is to obtain insights from our models and simulations for the development of new therapeutics. I am co-advised by David Mobley and Doug Tobias at UCI.

Science Writer

The Loh Down on Science

I write and edit scripts for the radio show and podcast, "The Loh Down on Science." The program explains the world of science with a dash of humor in less than two minutes. Broadcasted on Souther California Public Radio (KPCC) and National Public Radio (NPR).

See my LDOS bio page here.

Consultant

UC Irvine Graduate Resource Center

I work one-on-one with UCI graduate students, postdoctoral scholars, alumni, and staff to assist with communication skills. I've helped people prepare for exam presentations, general audience presentations, job interviews, English-speaking exams, and networking events.

Outreach volunteer
